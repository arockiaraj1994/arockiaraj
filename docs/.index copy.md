# Welcome to my website

Welcome to my website! This is a place where I share my thoughts, ideas, and experiences. I hope you find something interesting here.


## How I am looking for ...

### Understand

First understand the problem/process what ever it is. Even understand something how it works.

### Ideate

Think and generate multiple ideas

### Learn

Learn/Gain the knowledge to convert your ideas into reality. By reading, watching, listening, practicing, etc.

### Implement

Implement your ideas with your knowledge.

### Share

Share your knowledge/experience with others. It can be in the form of writing, speaking, teaching, etc. It is a way to learn and understand better. Of course, failure experiments as well

### Collaborate

Collaborate with others to solve the problem. It is a way to learn and understand better.
